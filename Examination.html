* Examinations *:

The academic year commences from March and continues till March of next year.               
      
There are 3 terms in an academic year:                                                            

o    Term 1 March to August                                                                   
o    Term 2 September to December                                                               
o   Term 3 January to March                                                                   
Note: An academic year may vary according to regional BISE requirements.

                        Results

Examination results are compiled, recorded and maintained in respective student files.
Results are usually put up on the Class Examination Result Sheet; copies of which are
authenticated by the SEC and communicated to the Controller Examinations.               
Results are obtained from the following tests and exams:                                               
o Monthly Tests                                                                                                           
o Mid-year Examination                                                                                              
o Final Examination                                                                                                     
The  result is  recorded  in  a  Progress Report and then  communicated to  respective 
students and parents.
