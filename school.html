Principal's Message:


                           It is my assurance that the first
and foremost objective to establish the Little Angles
Pilot School is to  produce  such  students  who are imparted  the  best  of  the  modern  excluding religious teachings to encourage in them spirit of patriotism and the devotion for their mother land.

Talented and the intelligent students are the richest  chattels  of  a  nation  and  it is very imperative to provide them all the best educational amenities. We believe that education is not an end but a means to an end.

In other words we do not educate children only for the underlying principle of  improving their  qualifications.  Our  purpose is to make them vigorous for life.  
So it  is very  important  to  choose  such  a system  of education  which  really
prepares children for future challenges. We do not believe in mechanical
education.

The school should not be  a  foundry  and the object should be  to  give every
pupil a  chance to grow.The school should be a garden and the educationist
should therefore  prepare  the  children  for  embellishing  their  individuality .
Educationist caters to the need of personality grooming and we are bigheaded
 to be  educationists.

                                                                                     Mrs.Shamim Amjad
                                                                                      (Principal)
